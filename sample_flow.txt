# AwaazBank — Sample Conversation Flow
# This shows a complete onboarding in Bhojpuri/Hindi

=== USER SENDS VOICE NOTE ===
🎙 "Hum SBI mein khata kholna chahtat hain"
   (Translation: "I want to open an account in SBI")

=== AWAAZBANK RESPONDS (voice) ===
🤖 "Namaste! Main AwaazBank hun, SBI ka AI sahayak.
    Khata kholne mein aapki madad karunga.
    Aap kaun sa khata kholna chahte hain?
    Savings Account ya Jan Dhan Account?"
   (Translation: "Hello! I'm AwaazBank, SBI's AI assistant.
    I'll help you open an account.
    Which account do you want?
    Savings Account or Jan Dhan Account?")

=== USER SENDS VOICE NOTE ===
🎙 "Jan Dhan khata chahiye"
   (Translation: "I want Jan Dhan account")

=== AWAAZBANK RESPONDS (voice) ===
🤖 "Bahut achha! Jan Dhan khata ke liye
    aapka Aadhaar number batayein."
   (Translation: "Great! Please tell me your
    Aadhaar number for Jan Dhan account.")

=== USER SENDS VOICE NOTE ===
🎙 "1234 5678 9012"

=== AWAAZBANK (sends OTP via UIDAI) ===
🤖 "Dhanyawaad. Aapke Aadhaar se jude mobile par
    OTP bheja gaya hai. Woh OTP batayein."
   (Translation: "Thank you. An OTP has been sent to the
    mobile linked to your Aadhaar. Please tell me that OTP.")

=== USER SENDS VOICE NOTE ===
🎙 "4 5 6 7 8 9"

=== AWAAZBANK (OTP verified ✅) ===
🤖 "OTP sahi hai! Ab ek selfie lijiye aur
    WhatsApp par bhejiye."
   (Translation: "OTP is correct! Now take a selfie
    and send it on WhatsApp.")

=== USER SENDS SELFIE IMAGE ===
📸 [selfie photo]

=== AWAAZBANK (liveness check ✅, account opened ✅) ===
🤖 "Badhai ho Ramji ji! 🎉
    Aapka SBI Jan Dhan khata khul gaya hai!
    Account number hai: SBI87654321
    IFSC code: SBIN0001234
    Yeh number save kar lijiye."
   (Translation: "Congratulations Ramji ji! 🎉
    Your SBI Jan Dhan account is now open!
    Account number: SBI87654321
    IFSC code: SBIN0001234
    Please save this number.")

=== TOTAL TIME: ~4 minutes ===
=== BRANCH VISIT REQUIRED: None ===
=== TYPING REQUIRED: None ===
